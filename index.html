About Us

Vipravrinda is a registered non-profit organization uniting the Trimathasta Kannada Brahmin community in America. We foster cultural connections, traditions, and shared values amongst our members. By organizing events and activities, we provide a platform for collaboration, support, and community growth. Vipravrinda is dedicated to preserving, practicing, and developing the Kannada Brahmin lifestyle while building a strong network for future generations. Through our efforts, we strengthen cultural identity and nurture a sense of belonging for those within the Kannada Brahmin diaspora.

Our Mission

To preserve, practice, and support Kannada Brahmin lifestyle, culture, and arts, and to foster welfare, religious, and education initiatives in service of our community.

Our Pillars

Networking

To build a strong global network of Kannada Brahmin families that align with our mission and to strengthen ties with Kannada Brahmin associations worldwide, enabling collaboration and participation in shared cultural and community events

Religious

To perform and support Kannada Brahmin religious activities, traditions, and beliefs.

Cultural

To promote cultural programs and arts, exchange artists and scholars worldwide, and celebrate the contributions of Kannada Brahmins to society and the country.

Seva

To perform selfless service to our community in accordance with our duty as Brahmins by providing a platform for performing funeral rituals for all Hindus and supporting education, health, and welfare initiatives for the Kannada Brahmin community.
